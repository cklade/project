Meet the Team

Cole Klade

Founder and Lead Photographer

Cole Klade is the founder and lead photographer behind Klade Photography. With experience in portraits, still photography, long exposure work, nature photography, and urban settings, he brings both artistic vision and technical precision to every session.

His approach is centered on making clients feel comfortable and confident, creating photographs that feel genuine, polished, and memorable.

Second Photographer

Assistant Photographer

Our second photographer expands the flexibility and coverage of each session, especially during events and larger shoots. With a strong eye for candid moments and subtle details, they help capture the parts of a story that might otherwise be missed.

Together, our team is committed to delivering beautiful imagery, professional service, and an experience clients can genuinely enjoy.
